# CIMB Digital Banking CLV & Sentiment Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes 45,524 customer reviews from the Octo Mobile application (Bank CIMB Niaga) to identify digital banking experience issues, customer dissatisfaction patterns, and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) risks.

Using NLP-based sentiment analysis and business analytics approaches, this project evaluates how digital friction impacts customer retention, engagement, and long-term business sustainability.

---

## Business Problem
As digital banking becomes the primary customer touchpoint, recurring issues such as login failures, transaction errors, and application instability can increase customer churn risk and reduce long-term customer value.

This project aims to quantify how negative digital experiences translate into Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) exposure and strategic business risk.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze customer sentiment from Octo Mobile reviews
- Identify major digital banking pain points
- Evaluate business impact on customer retention and CLV
- Generate strategic recommendations to improve digital banking experience

---

## Dataset
### Source
Google Play Store reviews of Octo Mobile (Bank CIMB Niaga)

### Data Scope
- 45,524 customer reviews
- Ratings: 1–3 stars
- Unstructured text data
- Review timestamps and app version information

---

## Methodology
### Analytical Process
1. Data Collection
2. Text Preprocessing
3. Sentiment Classification
4. Frequent Word & Phrase Analysis
5. Issue Categorization
6. CLV Risk Estimation
7. Strategic Business Analysis

### Techniques Used
- NLP Text Analytics
- Sentiment Analysis
- Customer Risk Analysis
- Business Impact Assessment
- CLV Estimation

---

## Key Findings
- 57.4% of reviews were classified as negative sentiment
- Login & access issues were the dominant pain point
- Transaction errors and feature instability significantly increased churn risk
- Estimated Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) at risk exceeded Rp33.5 billion
- Digital banking reliability strongly impacts customer trust and retention

---

## Strategic Insights
The analysis indicates that digital banking experience is not only an operational issue, but also a strategic revenue protection factor.

Persistent digital friction may:
- Increase customer churn probability
- Reduce transaction frequency
- Weaken customer loyalty
- Lower long-term profitability
